Jambalaya

Description

Jambalaya is a popular cajun dish combing meats, rice, and vegetables with delicious cajun spice!

Popular choices of meats include chicken, andouille sausage, and shrimp. Vegetables include the "holy trinity" of cajun cooking: celery, bell pepper, and onion. Tomato and garlic are also common ingredients.

Ingredients

Oil and seasoning

  • 3 tbsp cooking oil, divided
  • 2 tbsp cajun seasoning
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 tsp hot pepper sauce
  • 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce

Meat

  • 10 oz andouille sausage
  • 1 lb boneless skinless chicken breasts or thighs
  • 1 lb peeled and deveined raw shrimp

Vegetables

  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 2 stalks celery, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 14 oz can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 1/2 cup okra, thinly sliced

Other

  • 1 1/2 cup uncooked white rice
  • 3 cup chicken broth
